MCPS Sidekick

In my junior year of high school, my school district, MCPS, provides a grade viewer app that doesn't look very appealing. I created an alternative that is not only more visually appealing, but adds a key feature - a grade calculator. Students can add an assignment - for example a test - and calculate the score they need to improve or maintain their letter grade. Click to view some screenshots.
